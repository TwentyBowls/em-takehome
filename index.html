The Smallest and shallowest World's Major Oceans

The Arctic Ocean is the smallest and shallowest of the world's five major oceans. I is also known as the coldest of all the oceans. The international Hydrographic Oranization (IHO) recognizes it as an ocean, although some oceanographers call it the Arctic Sea. It is somethimes classified as an estuary of the Atlantic Ocean, and it is also seen as the northernmost part of the all-encompassing World Ocean.
